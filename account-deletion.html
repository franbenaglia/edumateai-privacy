EduMateAI – Account Deletion

If you want to delete your EduMateAI account and associated data, you can request it by contacting us.

How to request account deletion

  1. Send an email to: francisco.arcebenaglia@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject: EduMateAI Account Deletion Request
  3. Include the email address associated with your account.

What data will be deleted

When your request is processed, we will delete:

  • User account information
  • Uploaded study materials and processed content
  • Generated summaries and questions
  • User preferences

Data retention

Some limited information related to purchases may be retained temporarily if required for legal or financial compliance.

Processing time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 7 days.

If you have any questions, please contact: francisco.arcebenaglia@gmail.com
